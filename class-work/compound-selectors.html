Coffee Shop

This peice is a calm peice, portraying a window of a coffee shop just after the rain. The focus is on the window itself; the water droplets on the window, the metal work of its frame, the flower arrangement in the flower box, the brick of the wall. In the window itself, you can make out a blurry image of a chaotic series of events. Cups in the air, with their contents out in the air as well, customers falling, chairs on the ground, a waitress tripping over a cat, two other cats in view, one looking at the action and the other napping.
